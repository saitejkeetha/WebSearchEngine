Power BI suite provides multiple software, connector, and services - Power BI desktop, Power BI service based on Saas, and mobile Power BI apps available for different platforms. This tutorial covers all the important concepts in Power BI and provides a foundational understanding on how to use Power BI. This tutorial will give you enough understanding on Power BI, from where you can take yourself to a higher level of expertise. Prerequisites Before proceeding with this tutorial, you should be familiar with Microsoft Excel, data modeling, and have some knowledge of DAX language.
