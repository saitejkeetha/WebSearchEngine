By default, documents saved in Word 2010 are saved with the .docx extension. Microsoft Word can be used for the following purposes ? To create business documents having various graphics including pictures, charts, and diagrams. To store and reuse readymade content and formatted elements such as cover pages and sidebars. To create letters and letterheads for personal and business purpose. To design different documents such as resumes or invitation cards etc. To create a range of correspondence from a simple office memo to legal copies and reference documents.
