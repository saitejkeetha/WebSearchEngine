A - Inspection B - Repair C - Preventive Maintenance D - Breakdown Maintenance Answer : C Explanation Inspection Inspection is done to check the actual condition of a technical system. Preventive Maintenance Preventive maintenance is used to maintain high availability of the technical system. It includes maintenance planning and work scheduling activities for technical objects.
