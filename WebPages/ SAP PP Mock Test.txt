Mock Test I Mock Test II Mock Test III Mock Test IV SAP PP Mock Test I Q 1 - In which of the Production type, produce materials changed with each lot and costs are calculated as per orders and lots? A - Repetitive production B - Discrete Production C - Manufacturing Production D - Material Requirement Planning Answer : B Explanation Discrete production is known as industry where produce materials changed with each lot and costs are calculated as per orders and lots. In Repetitive production, product is not changed for longer time period and production is done as total quantity and not in the form of individual lots.
