Ads in Kids apps Ads are expected to be consistent with the maturity rating of the app or game. Ad serving can change over time so you should check what types of ads are being shown from time to time. If you see ads that aren't appropriate for the age group the app was designed for, report the ad to Google. Use parental controls to restrict mature content If you share your Android phone or tablet with others, including kids, you can turn on parental controls to block downloads or purchases of content based on the content maturity level. You can choose different parental control settings for each type of content, and for each device. Learn more about setting up parental controls.
