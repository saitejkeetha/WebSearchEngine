CBC 4 hrs · The curlers who will represent Team Canada at the 2022 Olympic and Paralympic Games in Beijing will be determined in Saskatoon this week but their uniforms have already been unveiled. Patrick Hunter, an Ojibe and two-spirit artist, created the designs in the style of Anishinaabe art. The jerseys feature seven trees for the seven grandfather teachings of the Anishinaabe culture on the front, a unique eagle feather for each of the four team members on the sides and a maple le...af on the back.
